## Round 3

### Storyteller (4.0s)

*I nod, tucking the photo away with stiff fingers* "Agreed. Whitmore's notes mentioned the chamber was in the sub-level. Said the water pipes were... 'conduits for resonance.' I didn't understand it then."

### Dr. Elena Marsh (7.5s)

*I freeze at the top of the stairwell, my flashlight beam joining yours to illuminate the dark substance coating the steps*

*I grip the stair railing, knuckles white*

"Elena, that dripping - it's not random. Listen..." *I count silently* "Every three seconds. Exactly. Like it's being... regulated."

*I turn to face you, my expression grim in the flashlight's glow*

"If someone - or something - is down there actively bleeding, we could be walking into a trap. But if it's Dr. Whitmore..." *I swallow hard* "We can't abandon him."

*I test the first step carefully, my shoe making a wet sound against the viscous surface*

"Stay close. Keep your light on the steps ahead of me. And Elena? If that dripping stops - or if it speeds up - we run. No hesitation, no looking back. Whatever's creating that rhythm knows we're here."

*I begin the descent, each step accompanied by that unnaturally precise dripping from below*

## Round 4

### Storyteller (3.9s)

*I follow, my own breath coming in shallow puffs that fog the air* "The blood... it's not pooling. It's following the grooves between the steps. Like channels."

### Dr. Elena Marsh (7.9s)

*I stop mid-step, one foot hovering above the next blood-slicked stone*

*I slowly turn my flashlight beam back up the stairwell we've descended*

"Elena, look at the blood trail behind us. It wasn't there when we started down." *My voice is tight with barely controlled fear* "The building isn't just influencing us - it's rewriting itself around us."

*The dripping shifts tempo slightly, becoming almost... expectant*

"Those etchings you found - they're not just matching the symbols. They're completing a pattern. Each step we take down is another line in whatever diagram this place is carving."

*I grip your arm*

"We have two choices. We can continue down and become part of whatever ritual this asylum is conducting, or we can try to break the pattern. But Elena... I don't think we can go back up. Not the same way we came."

*The dripping pauses for exactly one heartbeat, then resumes*

"It's waiting for us to decide."
